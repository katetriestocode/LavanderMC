Welcome to LavanderMC!

LavenderMC is a semi-vanilla minecraft server. With a focus of being transparent about our process, and working with our community, and staying community focused. In Lavender we have a wide range of extra content we are exited about, such as custom structures, custom terrain, and some of our own home grown plugins too. LavenderMC is a vibrant community, represented by our name. A true Semi-Vanilla survival server, that takes minecraft back to its core, with no pay to win features such as overpowered enchantments. In the words of our community "LavenderMC is a place where all players get to interact, but also build their own stories." So come hang out with us, on almost any version, bedrock or java, and bonus points if you use plasmo voice!
